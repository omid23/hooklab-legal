Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HookLab, a product operated by Strategy Trader Inc. ("HookLab", "we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the HookLab app and website (the "Service"). Strategy Trader Inc. is the data controller for the personal data described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. With Sign in with Apple you may choose to hide your email, in which case we receive a private relay address.
  • Content you provide: Video URLs, topics, instructions, and the scripts generated for you.
  • Usage and diagnostic data: Basic app interactions and technical diagnostics used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.
  • Payment data: Purchases are processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card details; we store a customer identifier and purchase history.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and provide the Service (generating scripts, saving your library).
  • To process purchases and manage credits and subscriptions.
  • To provide support and to help protect the security and reliability of the Service.

Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process your personal data on these legal bases: performance of a contract (to authenticate you, generate scripts, and process purchases); legitimate interests (to secure, maintain, and improve the Service and prevent abuse); legal obligation (to keep transaction and tax records); and consent, where we ask for it (you may withdraw consent at any time).

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information (California and similar states)

We do not sell your personal information for money, and we do not "share" it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or tracking. Because we do not sell or share personal information, we do not offer a "Do Not Sell or Share" opt-out; if this ever changes, we will update this policy and provide the required mechanism and notice.

Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of these rights: access to the data we hold about you, correction, deletion, portability, restriction of or objection to certain processing, opt-out of "sale"/"sharing", and withdrawal of consent. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. To exercise a right, email us from your account email address at omidbeigi23@gmail.com. We will verify your identity and respond within the period required by law. EEA/UK users may also lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority.

Data retention & deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time in the app under Settings → Delete Account. You may also request deletion by emailing us from your account email address. We will delete data we control within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records (for example, transaction and tax records held by our payment processor) and except for residual copies that may remain in backups for a limited time before being overwritten. Content you submit for AI processing is handled by our providers (OpenAI / Microsoft Azure) under their own retention terms.

We are based in the United States and use service providers located in the United States and other countries. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the Standard Contractual Clauses provided by our processors.

Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and restricted access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

The Service is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13; if we learn we have, we will delete it. Users in the EEA/UK must be at least the age of digital consent in their country (13–16 depending on the country) or have parental consent.
