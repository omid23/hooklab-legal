Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HookLab ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the HookLab app and website (the "Service").

Information we collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. With Sign in with Apple you may choose to hide your email, in which case we receive a private relay address.
  • Content you provide: Video URLs, topics, instructions, and the scripts generated for you.
  • Usage data: Basic app interactions and diagnostics used to operate and improve the Service.
  • Payment data: Purchases are processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card details; we store a customer identifier and purchase history.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and provide the Service (generating scripts, saving your library).
  • To process purchases and manage credits and subscriptions.
  • To provide support and to maintain the security and reliability of the Service.

Third-party services

We share the minimum data necessary with providers that help us run the Service: Apple and Google (authentication), Stripe (payments), and AI processing providers (OpenAI / Microsoft Azure) to generate scripts from the content you submit. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing omidbeigi23@gmail.com.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information and we do not use it for third-party advertising or tracking.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
